// 06 · Editing Process

The Production Journey

Every frame is engineered through a precise 9-stage cinematic pipeline.

01

Discovery

Deep dive into your vision, brand, audience, and objectives. We align on the creative direction before a single frame is cut.

⏱ 2-3 daysNotionZoom
DELIVERABLES: Creative brief, moodboard, timeline
02

Script

Narrative architecture — voiceover, shot lists, and pacing mapped out. Every story needs a blueprint.

⏱ 1-2 daysChatGPTFinal Draft
DELIVERABLES: Script, shot list, voiceover draft
03

Editing

The cut takes shape. Rhythm, pacing, and story structure are refined until the narrative breathes on its own.

⏱ 3-7 daysPremiere ProFinal Cut
DELIVERABLES: Rough cut, fine cut, locked edit
04

Motion Graphics

Typography, transitions, lower thirds, and kinetic visuals elevate the edit into a cinematic experience.

⏱ 3-5 daysAfter EffectsCavalry
DELIVERABLES: Title sequences, animations, VFX
05

Color Grading

Color sets emotion. Cinematic LUTs and custom grades transform raw footage into mood-driven visuals.

⏱ 1-2 daysDaVinci Resolve
DELIVERABLES: Color-graded master, LUT pack
06

Sound Design

Sound is 50% of the experience. Music selection, foley, mixing, and mastering create the sonic landscape.

⏱ 1-3 daysAuditionLogic Pro
DELIVERABLES: Mixed audio, stems, master
07

Export

Multi-format renders optimized for every platform — YouTube, Instagram, TV, cinema, web.

⏱ 1 dayMedia Encoder
DELIVERABLES: 4K, 1080p, social cuts, ProRes
08

Client Review

Frame-accurate feedback via Frame.io. Timecoded notes, revisions, and approvals in one collaborative flow.

⏱ 1-2 daysFrame.io
DELIVERABLES: Review link, revision rounds
09

Final Delivery

Archived masters, source files, and optimized exports delivered via secure cloud with full documentation.

⏱ 1 dayGoogle Drive
DELIVERABLES: Final masters, archive, license
